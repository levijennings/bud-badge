# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Bud Badge (operated by DVLMNT) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies, and how you can manage your preferences.

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that contain information about your activity on our website and within the Bud Badge platform. Cookies can be "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent" cookies (stored until they expire).

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### 2.1 Essential Cookies
These cookies are required for Bud Badge to function properly and include:
- Session management and user authentication
- Security tokens to prevent unauthorized access
- Account information and login credentials
- Protection against fraud and abuse
- Language and interface preferences

**These cannot be disabled without affecting core functionality.**

### 2.2 Analytics Cookies
These help us understand how users interact with Bud Badge:
- **Sentry:** Error tracking and crash reporting
- **PostHog:** Usage analytics and feature adoption
- **Custom Analytics:** Tracking of training completion rates, module access, and assessment performance

These cookies help us identify technical issues and improve the Service.

### 2.3 Functional Cookies
These cookies improve your experience by remembering:
- Your account preferences and settings
- Training progress and bookmarks within modules
- Dashboard filters and view preferences
- Browser zoom level and display preferences
- Notification settings

### 2.4 Performance Cookies
These cookies measure how well Bud Badge performs:
- Page load times
- Server response times
- User interface responsiveness
- Feature performance metrics

## 4. How to Manage Your Cookies

### 4.1 Browser Settings
Most browsers allow you to control cookies through settings:
- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
- **Edge:** Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Clear browsing data

You can choose to:
- Accept all cookies
- Accept only essential cookies
- Delete existing cookies
- Set specific cookie preferences by website

### 4.2 Cookie Consent Banner
When you first access Bud Badge, we display a cookie consent banner allowing you to:
- **Accept All:** Accept all cookies
- **Essential Only:** Accept only essential cookies
- **Customize:** Choose specific cookie categories

Your preference is saved in a consent cookie.

### 4.3 Third-Party Opt-Out
You can opt out of some third-party analytics services:
- **PostHog:** https://posthog.com/docs/faq#how-to-opt-out-of-posthog
- **Sentry:** Disabling analytics in browser settings

### 4.4 Do Not Track
If your browser includes a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting and you enable it, we will limit analytics cookie collection.

## 5. What Happens if You Disable Cookies?

If you disable essential cookies, Bud Badge may not function properly, including:
- Inability to log in or maintain sessions
- Loss of account functionality
- Inability to complete training or assessments
- Loss of preferences and settings

Disabling analytics cookies will not significantly affect functionality but may prevent us from identifying technical issues and improving the Service.

## 6. Cookie Retention

- **Session cookies:** Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies:** Typically retained for 1 year unless cleared earlier
- **Consent cookie:** Retained for 1 year (refreshed if you adjust preferences)
- **Stripe cookies:** Retained per Stripe's requirements

## 7. Security and Privacy

7.1 **Secure Transmission:** Cookies are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

7.2 **No Sensitive Data:** Cookies do not contain passwords, payment information, or other highly sensitive data.

7.3 **Privacy:** For more information about how we collect and use cookie data, see our Privacy Policy.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Cookies are stored on your device but data collected through cookies may be transferred internationally. See our Privacy Policy for details.
