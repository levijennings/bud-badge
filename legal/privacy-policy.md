# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

DVLMNT ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the Bud Badge platform and related services (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using Bud Badge, you consent to our data practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Employer Account Information:** Name, company name, address, email, phone number, payment information (processed through Stripe), and organizational details.

**Employee Information:** Name, email, employee ID, hire date, training assignments, and department/role information.

**Training Data:** Completion dates, module scores, assessment results, time spent on training, responses to training content, and certification records.

**Communications:** Content of emails, support tickets, and inquiries you send us.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data:** Pages accessed, features used, time spent on each module, clicks and interactions, assessment attempt data, and API usage patterns.

**Device Information:** Device type, operating system, browser, IP address, and device identifier.

**Cookies and Tracking Technologies:** We use cookies to track sessions, remember preferences, and analyze usage patterns.

### 1.3 Third-Party Data Sources

We may receive information from:
- Payment processors (Stripe)
- Analytics providers
- Authentication services

## 2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 **Service Delivery:** We use information to:
- Provide and maintain Bud Badge
- Process subscriptions and payments
- Manage employee accounts and training records
- Generate completion certificates and reports
- Provide customer support

2.2 **Training and Compliance:** We use information to:
- Track employee training completion
- Generate compliance reports
- Maintain required records for regulatory purposes
- Identify training gaps and recommend modules
- Monitor assessment performance

2.3 **Communication:** We use your email to send:
- Service notifications and updates
- Billing information and invoices
- Training reminders and completion updates
- Customer support responses
- Optional newsletters and product updates

2.4 **Analytics and Improvement:** We analyze usage patterns to:
- Understand how users interact with the Service
- Improve training effectiveness
- Identify technical issues and bugs
- Enhance features and content

2.5 **Safety and Legal Compliance:** We use information to:
- Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
- Enforce these Terms
- Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
- Respond to regulatory inquiries
- Protect the rights and safety of users

2.6 **Marketing:** With your consent, we may use information for:
- Promotional emails about new features
- Industry webinars or events
- Case studies (with anonymized or aggregated data)

## 3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 **Employee Records:** Employers have access to their employees' training records, including names, completion dates, scores, and certifications. Employees have access to their own records.

3.2 **Service Providers:** We share information with service providers who perform services on our behalf:
- Supabase (database hosting and authentication)
- Stripe (payment processing)
- Email delivery services
- Analytics providers
- Cloud storage providers

Service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

3.3 **Third-Party Services:**
- **Stripe:** Payment processing and billing
- **Supabase:** Database hosting and authentication
- **Sentry:** Error tracking and monitoring
- **PostHog:** Analytics and feature usage

3.4 **Regulatory Authorities:** We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or legal process. This may include:
- Training records and certifications
- Employee completion data
- Account information
- Usage data

3.5 **Business Transfers:** If DVLMNT is acquired or merges, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

3.6 **Aggregated and Anonymized Data:** We may share aggregated, anonymized data about training effectiveness, completion rates, and industry trends. This data does not identify individuals.

3.7 **No Sale:** We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 **Storage Location:** Your data is stored on servers managed by Supabase, which may be located in various jurisdictions, including the United States.

4.2 **Security Measures:** We implement industry-standard security, including:
- Encryption in transit (SSL/TLS)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments
- Secure password hashing

However, no security is 100% effective.

4.3 **Data Retention:** We retain your information as follows:
- Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
- Terminated accounts: Records retained for legal compliance (typically 3-7 years)
- Payment information: Retained per Stripe's requirements
- Usage data: Typically retained for 12 months
- Regulatory records: Retained as required by law

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 Access and Portability
You have the right to access your personal information and receive it in a portable format. To request this, email levi@dvlmnt.com with "Data Access Request" in the subject line.

### 5.2 Correction and Deletion
You have the right to correct inaccurate information. Note that some information may need to be retained for legal/regulatory purposes. Email levi@dvlmnt.com with "Data Correction" in the subject line.

### 5.3 Opt-Out
You may opt out of:
- Marketing emails: Click unsubscribe in emails or contact us
- Non-essential communications: Manage preferences in account settings

Note: Service notifications and compliance records cannot be opt-out.

### 5.4 CCPA Rights (California Residents)
If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Delete personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
- Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise CCPA rights, contact levi@dvlmnt.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

### 5.5 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)
If you are an EU resident, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:
- Access to your personal data
- Correction of inaccurate data
- Erasure ("right to be forgotten," subject to legal hold)
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent
- Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority

To exercise GDPR rights, contact levi@dvlmnt.com.
